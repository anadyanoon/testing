# STILL — a late-night companion for the nervous system

For anyone carrying a difficult past: dysfunctional families, the hypervigilance
and people-pleasing that follow, shame spirals, loneliness, burnout, and the
emotional exhaustion of just getting through.

No backend. No login. No tracking. Works offline. Free forever.

---

## What it is

A cinematic, dark, glassmorphic space you open when the feeling hits.
You don't pick a "mood" to perform — you tell it what's *here right now*,
and it meets you there.

## The six rooms (organised by feeling)

| Room | For the moments when… |
|------|----------------------|
| **Anxious** | your body won't settle, hypervigilance, bracing |
| **Heavy** | everything is too much, burnout, weighed down |
| **Not enough** | the shame voice is loud, conditional-love wounds |
| **Lonely** | unseen, the ache of distance learned in childhood |
| **Guilty** | the people-pleasing, owing everyone, can't say no |
| **Numb** | gone quiet inside, can't feel, dissociation |

Each room gives you:
- a **breathing orb** with in/hold/out cues, paced to your setting
- **reflective reframes** written for adult children of dysfunction —
  swipe or tap through them
- a **grounding exercise** (5-4-3-2-1, gentle contact, returning to yourself…)
- a **private journal** prompt — saved only on your device, read by no one

## The "I'm spiralling" button

Always at the bottom. One tap drops you into a crisis-grounding flow —
longer-exhale breathing and steadying lines for the worst moments.

## Sound

Six ambient soundscapes (rain, ocean, wind, fire, warm drone, 432 Hz),
all generated live in the browser. No files, no copyright, infinite, free.
Layer and mix them. Available from any room or the ♪ button.

---

## Customise without code  (the ✦ button)

- rename the app
- change the welcome line
- set your own breathing pace
- **add your own reframes** — the lines *you* needed to hear

Everything saves to your device. Make a version for yourself,
or a gentler one for someone you love.

## Deploy free in 2 minutes

**Netlify Drop** — go to netlify.com/drop, drag this folder on. Done.
**GitHub Pages** — upload the 3 files, Settings → Pages → main branch.
**Vercel** — vercel.com → Add New → upload folder.

## Install on any device

After visiting the live URL:
- **iPhone/iPad**: Share → Add to Home Screen
- **Android**: Menu → Install app
- **Desktop**: install icon in the Chrome address bar

Works fully offline after the first visit.

---

## A note on safety

STILL is a companion for hard nights, not a replacement for care.
If you are in crisis or thinking about harming yourself, please reach out
to a crisis line in your country or someone you trust. You deserve real support,
not just an app.
